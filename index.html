Find Answers to Your Water Donation Questions

Helping to bring clean water to every person who needs it.

How does my donation help?

Your donation goes directly towards funding clean water projects in communities that lack access to safe water.

Can I donate in someone's honor?

Yes, you can make a donation in honor of a loved one, and we will send them a personalized acknowledgment of your gift.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

All donations are tax-deductible to the full extent permitted by law. We will provide you with a donation receipt for your records.

How are the water projects implemented?

We partner with local organizations to build sustainable, community-owned water projects around the world.

How can I get involved beyond donating?

There are many ways to get involved, including volunteering, starting a fundraiser, or becoming an advocate for clean water.
